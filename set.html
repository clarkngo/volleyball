The Set

What it is:

The set is the second touch in a rally, and it's designed to prepare the ball for an attacker. The setter uses their fingertips to push the ball high and into a position where a spiker can hit it over the net.

Analogy for Beginners:

A setter is like a "quarterback" in American football. They take the pass and then launch the ball to the perfect spot for their attacker to score, just like a quarterback throws the ball to a receiver.
