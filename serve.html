The Serve

What it is:

The serve is the action that starts every rally in volleyball. A player stands behind the end line and hits the ball, attempting to send it over the net and into the opponent's court.

Analogy for Beginners:

Think of the serve as a "grand opening" for the point. It's like the opening pitch in baseball or the first shot in a game of pool. Your goal is to start the game in a way that gives your team an advantage.
