The Block

What it is:

A block is a defensive action where a player jumps at the net to stop the opponent's spike. The goal is to either stop the ball completely or deflect it to a teammate.

Analogy for Beginners:

Blocking is like being a "wall" at the net. Your job is to jump up and create a barrier that the other team can't hit through. You're defending your court by putting up a shield right where they want to attack.
