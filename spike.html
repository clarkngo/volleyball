The Spike (or Hit)

What it is:

The spike is the most exciting and powerful offensive action. The player jumps and hits the ball with force over the net into the opponent's court, trying to score a point.

Analogy for Beginners:

Spiking is like "throwing a ball downwards" as hard as you can, but you use an open hand to hit it. Imagine you're on a step ladder and you want to throw a ball straight down over a fence. That's the motion and goal of a spike.
