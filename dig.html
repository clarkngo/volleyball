The Dig

What it is:

A dig is a defensive move to save a hard-driven spike from the opponent. It's a reactive, often sprawling, move to prevent the ball from hitting the floor.

Analogy for Beginners:

Digging is like being a "goalie" in soccer. You have to react in a split second to a powerful shot and do whatever it takes to keep the ball from getting past you and hitting the ground. It's about quick reflexes and determination.
